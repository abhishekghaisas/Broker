FIELD MANUAL

            
Neural Operative Extraction Protocol — Briefing Dossier


            
▸ MISSION

            
Reach The Extraction Rooftop and evacuate. The shuttle cannot land until you possess the Syndicate Decryption Key, purchasable at The Black Market for 400 credits.


            
▸ HOW TO PLAY

            
    
                
  • Speak to NOVA — talk naturally; NOVA is your in-implant AI guide and game master.
    • 
                
  • Speak clearly and try not to speak too fast — the neural link transcribes your voice; rushed or slurred speech can cause it to mishear commands.
    • 
                
  • Solve terminals — when a hacking puzzle appears, type your answer in the override field and submit.
    • 
                
  • Watch the HUD — callsign, location, health, and credits update live.
    • 
            


            
▸ LOCATIONS

            
    
                
  • 🌆 Neon District — Gritty, gang-ridden slum. Engage NPCs in Negotiations to earn credits. Risky to linger.
    • 
                
  • 🛡️ The Safehouse — Secure, encrypted bunker. No hostiles can track you. A safe place to regroup.
    • 
                
  • 🏢 Syndicate Tower — Heavily guarded stronghold. Run Data Slicing hacks for credits. Patrols deal ambient damage — and it gets worse once you've hacked a terminal there.
    • 
                
  • 🛒 The Black Market — Underground bazaar. The only place to buy the Syndicate Decryption Key (400 credits).
    • 
                
  • 🚁 The Extraction Rooftop — The only way off the map. 🔒 LOCKED until you are holding the Syndicate Decryption Key — you cannot travel here before then.
    • 
            


            
▸ ACTIONS

            
    
                
  • 🧭 Travel (Tactical Routing) — Move between locations. Choose a safe route or a hazardous smuggler's route that risks damage for bonus credits.
    • 
                
  • 💻 Data Slicing (Syndicate Tower only) — Solve a logic/hacking puzzle. Success pays credits; failure locks you out and costs credits.
    • 
                
  • 🗣️ Negotiations (Neon District only) — Pick a tactic: Intimidation, Flattery, or Logic. Success earns credits; repeated failures anger the NPC and cost health.
    • 
                
  • 💳 Purchase (Black Market) — Buy the Syndicate Decryption Key once you have 400 credits.
    • 
            


            
▸ SURVIVAL

            
Hostile zones inflict random patrol/system damage. If your health hits 0, the mission ends in failure. Use The Safehouse to lie low and manage risk.


            
▸ PATH TO EXTRACTION

            
    
                
  1. Earn 400 credits via negotiations, hacks, and risky routes.
    2. 
                
  2. Travel to The Black Market and buy the Syndicate Decryption Key.
    3. 
                
  3. With the key in hand, The Extraction Rooftop unlocks — travel there.
    4. 
                
  4. Board the shuttle to win.
